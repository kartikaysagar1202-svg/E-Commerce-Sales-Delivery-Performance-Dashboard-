# E-Commerce Sales & Delivery Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Short Description
This Power BI project analyzes **sales performance and delivery efficiency** for an e-commerce business in Brazil.  
For example, the dashboard helps answer questions like:
- Which states generate the most customers and revenue?
- How long do deliveries take across different regions?
- Are increasing orders affecting delivery performance?

---

## Tech Stack / Tools Used
- Power BI Desktop  
- Power Query (data cleaning & transformation)  
- DAX (KPIs, measures, calculated fields)  
- Data Modeling  
- Interactive Visualizations & Slicers  

---

## Data Source
- Dataset sourced from **Kaggle**
- Public e-commerce transactional data related to orders, customers, products, payments, and delivery timelines

---

## Features & Highlights

### Business Problem
An e-commerce company needs visibility into:
- Overall sales performance
- Customer distribution across regions
- Delivery delays and fulfillment efficiency  
Without a centralized dashboard, identifying bottlenecks and growth opportunities becomes difficult.

---

### Goal of the Dashboard
- Track key business KPIs such as revenue, orders, customers, and delivery performance  
- Identify high-performing regions and product categories  
- Analyze delivery timelines and on-time delivery rates  
- Support data-driven decision-making for operations and logistics  

---

### Key Visuals Walkthrough
- **KPI Cards:** Total Customers, Total Orders, Total Revenue, AOV, On-Time Delivery Rate  
- **Bar Charts:** Top customer locations by state, revenue by product category  
- **Map Visual:** Average delivery time by state across Brazil  
- **Donut Chart:** Order distribution by payment method  
- **Line Chart:** Monthly revenue trend  
- **Combo Chart:** Order growth vs average delivery days  
- **Delivery Distribution:** Orders grouped by delivery time ranges  

---

### Business Impact & Insights
- São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro are the strongest customer and revenue contributors  
- Majority of orders are delivered within **6–10 days**, indicating efficient logistics  
- Remote states experience higher delivery times, highlighting optimization opportunities  
- Revenue shows a consistent upward trend while delivery efficiency remains stable  
- Credit Card is the dominant payment method, guiding payment strategy decisions
